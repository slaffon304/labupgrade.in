Yes. We work with clients from Moldova and other countries, using online tools, project management and digital communication for efficient collaboration.

Yes. Lab Upgrade works full-cycle: audit, strategy, technical implementation, automation, CRM/AI integration and team training.

Payment is made in stages, depending on the project. After audit and solution definition, we establish a clear implementation plan and transparent costs.
